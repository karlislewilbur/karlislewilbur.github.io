Maple Frosted Cupcakes

Leonardo da Vinci once said "simplicity is the ultimate sophistication". The design of these maple frosted cupcakes stand to prove this very point with their elegant twist on a classic.

Supplies

  • 1 Dozen cooled cupcakes (any flavor)
  • Vanilla frosting
  • Maple syrup
  • A piping bag
  • An icing tip (of your choosing)
  • Strawberry fruit leather
  • A knife
  • A cutting board

Steps

  1. Mix vanilla frosting with 2-3 tablespoons of maple syrup. Put maple frosting in an icing bag with a icing tip of your choosing and set aside.
  2. Unwrap cupcakes.
  3. In a swirling motion frost the cupcakes with the maple frosting
  4. Using the cutting diagram in the photo above, cut strips of strawberry fruit leather into bite-sized hearts
  5. Place the heart shaped fruit leather pieces on top of the frosted cupcakes.
  6. Enjoy!

For Extra Flair ...

Tie a ribbon or lace around the base of the cupcake to give
it an antique, homemade feel.
