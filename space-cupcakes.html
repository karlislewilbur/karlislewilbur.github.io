Galaxy Cupcakes

Nothing can eclipse these cupcakeâ€™s out-of-this-world design. With hand-piped chocolate decorations and whimsical frosting these cupcakes are sure to be a stellar edition to any occasion.

Supplies

  • 1 Dozen cooled cupcakes (any flavor)
  • 1 dark colored pencil or marker
  • Sticky notes
  • 1 sheet pan
  • Wax paper
  • Tape
  • 1 package of chocolate melts
  • Vanilla frosting
  • Blue food coloring
  • Icing spatula
  • Piping bags (plastic or cloth)
  • Star-Shaped or large round icing tip

Steps

  1. Using a dark colored pencil or marker draw a collection of moon and star shapes on 16 sticky notes (1 for each cupcake and 4 extra in case of breakage)
  2. Line up sticky notes on a sheet pan then place a sheet of wax paper over the pan. Secure the paper with tape. Set aside.
  3. In a microwavable bowl melt 2 cups of chocolate melts. Then carefully pour the melted chocolates into a piping bag.
  4. Using the sticky notes beneath as a guide, draw star/moon shapes with chocolate on the wax paper. Once done place the sheet pan in the fridge to harden.
  5. In a mixing bowl, mix 3-4 drops of blue food coloring into vanilla frosting. Scoop blue frosting into a piping bag fitted with a star or large round icing tip.
  6. Frost cupcakes using a swirling motion. Then hardened chocolate decoration on top as a finishing touch.
  7. Enjoy!

For Extra Flair ...

lightly mix purple frosting along with the blue frosting in the piping bag. This will produce another swirl of color and give your cupcakes an extra galactic touch.
