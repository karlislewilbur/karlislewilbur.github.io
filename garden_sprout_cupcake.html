Garden Sprout Cupcakes

Although the breathtaking foliage of springtime is fleeting the easy to make, botanical design of these cupcakes will have you recalling the exciting, budding begins of the season all year long.

Supplies

  • 1 Dozen cooled chocolate (or devil’s food cake) cupcakes
  • Chocolate frosting
  • ½ a package of crushed Oreo cookies (or an equivalent chocolate cookie)
  • Vanilla frosting
  • Green and yellow food coloring
  • Icing spatula
  • Piping bags (plastic or cloth)
  • Leaf shaped icing tip
  • Paper
  • colored pencils
  • Scissors
  • Tape
  • Toothpicks

Steps

  1. Using an icing spatula or knife cover the tops of the cupcakes with a thin layer of chocolate frosting.
  2. Roll the top of the frosted cupcakes in crushed Oreos.
  3. Mix 3 drops of green food coloring into vanilla frosting. Then mix thoroughly to create green frosting.
  4. Fill a piping bag (with a leaf icing tip attached) with the green frosting.
  5. Using the green frosting pipe simple sprout shapes on top of each cupcake. (If stuck on what to pipe use references of bean sprouts).
  6. Finally, using the art supplies listed above, create a garden sign for each cupcake featuring a different vegatable-to-be. Then stick the signs between the frosting sprouts previously made with the green frosting.
  7. Enjoy!

For Extra Flair ...

place finished cupcakes in minature terra cotta flower pots.
You can find these pots at your local crafting supply store.
